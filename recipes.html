Garlicky Kale Salad

A delicious blend of garlic and spices covering crispy chickpeas on a bed of fresh summer kale.

  • One head of garlic
  • Salt
  • One can of chickpeas
  • A heaping serving of kale

Italian Caesar

A twist on the classic Caesar Salad with dandelion and chicory roots, fresh parmigiano reggiano, and homemade dressing that will be sure to please even the pickiest palate.

  • One bunch each of dandelion and chicory roots
  • 2 oz fresh parmigiano reggiano
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
